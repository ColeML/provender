Cranberry-Walnut Chicken Salad Sandwiches

Serves 8 · 15 min total

delino-cookkid-friendlyleftover-friendlysandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 cup rotisserie chicken — cubed (about 1 chicken)
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 Tbsp honey Dijon mustard
  • 1 cup Swiss cheese — shredded
  • ½ cup celery — chopped
  • ½ cup dried cranberries — have on hand
  • ¼ cup walnuts — chopped, have on hand
  • ½ tsp dried parsley
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • 8 leaves lettuce
  • 16 slices sandwich bread — plain (white or wheat)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the mayonnaise, honey Dijon mustard, and pepper. Stir in the chicken, Swiss cheese, celery, cranberries, walnuts, and parsley until evenly coated.
  2. For best flavor, cover and chill a few hours so the salad settles.
  3. Lay lettuce on 8 slices of bread and top each with about ½ cup of the chicken salad. Add the top slice, cut, and serve.
