Cornbread Muffins (potluck, 2 trays)

Serves 24 · 28 min total

breadsidepotluckovenbaking

Ingredients

  • 2 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 2 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2 Tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cup milk
  • 2 large egg
  • ½ cup cooking oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F and line or grease two standard 12-cup muffin tins (24 wells).
  2. In a large bowl, stir together the cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, eggs, and oil.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry and stir just until moistened — a few lumps are fine. Don't overmix.
  5. Divide the batter among the 24 wells, filling each about ¾ full. Bake 16–18 minutes, until the tops are golden and a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool a few minutes, then turn out. Makes 24 muffins (two trays) for the potluck.
