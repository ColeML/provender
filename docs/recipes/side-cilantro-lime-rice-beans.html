Cilantro-Lime Rice & Black Beans

Serves 8 · 25 min total

sidemexicanrice-cooker

Ingredients

  • 2 cup long grain rice
  • 2 can black beans — 15 oz each
  • 1 lime
  • ¼ bunch cilantro

Steps

  1. Cook rice in the rice cooker; fluff with lime juice and chopped cilantro.
  2. Warm drained black beans on the stovetop.
