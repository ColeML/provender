Smash Burgers

Serves 12 · 40 min total

americanbeefgriddleblackstonekid-friendly

Ingredients

  • 3 lb ground beef
  • 12 Tbsp frozen salted butter — grated, divided
  • 3 Tbsp burger seasoning — homemade or store-bought
  • 12 slices American cheese
  • 12 burger buns
  • 12 leaves iceberg lettuce
  • 3 tomato — sliced into thin rounds
  • 1 small red onion — sliced into thin rounds

Steps

  1. Divide the ground beef into 12 equal portions. Grate about ½ Tbsp frozen butter onto each, wrap the meat around it, and roll into a ball. Chill until ready to cook.
  2. Preheat the Blackstone griddle on high until ripping hot.
  3. Place the beef balls on the griddle, sprinkle each with burger seasoning, and smash flat with a sturdy spatula. Hold the smash for a few seconds.
  4. Cook undisturbed about 2 minutes, until the edges are crisp and the top changes color, then flip. Lay a slice of American cheese on each and cook 1–2 minutes more.
  5. Butter the bun cut-sides and toast them right on the griddle until golden.
  6. Build the burgers with onion, tomato, and lettuce. Serve hot. Makes 12 — for 4 adults + 2 kids.
