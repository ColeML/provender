Slow Cooker Pulled Pork

Serves 8 · 255 min total

bbqporkslow-cookerkid-friendlyleftover-friendlysandwich

Ingredients

  • 3 lb pork butt — cut into large chunks
  • 1 yellow onion — cut into chunks
  • 4 cloves garlic — roughly chopped
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 bottle BBQ sauce — about 18 oz, for serving
  • 8 hamburger buns

Steps

  1. Combine the spice rub: paprika, smoked paprika, brown sugar, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cut the pork butt into large chunks and toss with the spice rub until evenly coated.
  3. Add the onion to the bottom of the slow cooker. Place the seasoned pork on top, then scatter the chopped garlic over it.
  4. Cover and cook on high 4 hours or low 8 hours, until the pork shreds easily with a fork (add an hour if needed).
  5. Shred the pork with two forks and toss with BBQ sauce to taste.
  6. Pile onto buns and serve. For crispy edges, broil the shredded pork a few minutes first — skip on a hot day.
