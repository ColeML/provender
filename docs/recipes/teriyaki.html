Instant Pot Teriyaki Chicken and Rice

Serves 8 · 35 min total

asiankid-friendlyinstant-potquickleftover-friendly

Ingredients

  • 1⅓ lb chicken breast — boneless skinless
  • 4 clove garlic — minced
  • 2 tsp fresh ginger — grated
  • 3 cup jasmine rice — uncooked
  • 24 oz frozen stir fry vegetables
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • 4 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 4 green onion — sliced

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into ½–¾-inch pieces.
  2. Set the Instant Pot to Sauté (normal). Add 1 Tbsp cooking oil and the chicken; cook 2–3 minutes until the outside is no longer pink. Add the garlic and ginger and stir 30 seconds.
  3. In a small bowl, stir together the soy sauce, brown sugar, and toasted sesame oil.
  4. Rinse the jasmine rice until the water runs clear. Add it to the pot with 2½ cups water and the teriyaki sauce. Stir well and scrape any browned bits off the bottom (prevents a burn warning).
  5. Secure the lid, set the valve to Sealing, and Pressure Cook (Manual) on High for 4 minutes.
  6. When it finishes, let the pressure release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick-release any remaining pressure.
  7. Open the lid, add the frozen stir-fry vegetables, replace the lid (no pressure), and let sit 5 minutes to steam through. Fluff and stir gently.
  8. Top with sliced green onions and serve.
