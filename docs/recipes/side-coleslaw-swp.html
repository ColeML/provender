Coleslaw (celery seed)

Serves 8 · 80 min total

sideno-cookkid-friendly

Ingredients

  • 1 bag coleslaw mix — 14 oz, pre-shredded cabbage & carrot
  • ⅔ cup mayonnaise
  • 1⅓ Tbsp white vinegar
  • ⅔ Tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2⅔ tsp granulated sugar
  • ⅔ tsp celery seed — don't skip — makes the slaw
  • salt — to taste
  • black pepper — to taste

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk the dressing: mayonnaise, white vinegar, apple cider vinegar, sugar, celery seed, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  2. Add the coleslaw mix and toss until evenly coated.
  3. Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving to let the flavors blend.
