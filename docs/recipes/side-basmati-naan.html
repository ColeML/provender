Basmati Rice & Naan

Serves 8 · 25 min total

sideindianrice-cooker

Ingredients

  • 3 cup basmati rice — uncooked
  • 8 pieces naan
  • ¼ bunch cilantro — optional garnish

Steps

  1. Rinse the basmati rice until the water runs clear, then cook in the rice cooker (about 3 cups rice to 4½ cups water).
  2. Warm the naan in a dry skillet or on the griddle. Fluff the rice, garnish with cilantro, and serve under the butter chicken.
